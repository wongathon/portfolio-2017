About

I'm a full stack developer with a varied background. I've worked in advertising for Hollywood and in NYC, worked for NGO startups focusing on international devlelopment and water access, helped jumpstart the Swedish brand Fjallraven as a PR Operations guy, and as a Implementation Consultant for Epic Systems, the largest medical e-records provider in the US.

I'm currently living and actively working in the Washington DC Metro Area as a defense contractor and a freelance coder, making cool research and admin tools for my friends and colleagues. If I'm not coding, you can find me gymnastic strength training, biking, skiing, or rockclimbing, and either singing at the Karaoke Bar or as a tenor lead in rank with the Washington Men's Camerata.
